# 🎨 BrandCraft.ai

## Your Vibe = Your Brand

Describe the vibe, get the brand. AI-powered brand identity generator. No backend needed - everything runs in your browser!

## ✨ Features

- 🎨 **Dynamic Logo** - Unique DiceBear avatars based on your vibe
- 🎨 **Smart Color Palette** - AI-powered color selection based on keywords
- ✍️ **Perfect Font Pairing** - Automatic font recommendations
- 📦 **ZIP Download** - Download your complete brand kit (logo, colors, fonts)
- ⚡ **Complete Brand in 30 Seconds** - Fast and free

## 🎯 How It Works

1. Describe your brand vibe (e.g., "Modern coffee shop with industrial vibe")
2. AI generates a unique color palette and font pairing
3. Download your complete brand kit as a ZIP file

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- HTML5/CSS3
- Vanilla JavaScript
- Glassmorphism Design
- DiceBear Avatar API
